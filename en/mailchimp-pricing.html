Mailchimp Pricing & Plans

This page shows the current pricing and plans for Mailchimp, one of the most popular email marketing platforms.

Mailchimp Plans

  • Free – Limited features for small lists
  • Essentials – Starting from Mailchimp’s entry paid plan
  • Standard – Advanced automation and reporting
  • Premium – For large audiences and advanced needs

Pricing depends on the number of contacts and selected features. Always check the official website for the latest details.
